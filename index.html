About Me

Adam Batson is a Software Developer residing in Ottawa, Ontario. A graduate of the Software Engineering program at Carleton University, he combines a passion for technology with critical thinking and software development skills. He is currently seeking employment as a Software Developer in the Ottawa area.

Adam has experience developing applications in both web and desktop environments. Some of his favourite tools include Java, Python, Ruby on Rails, C#, and Git. He loves experimenting with and learning new tools.

Adam was born and raised in the small mining town of Labrador City, Newfoundland and Labrador. His hobbies include cross country skiing, running, biking, and computer programming.
